Bolt CMS automatically pre-loads your entire site from Google App Engine. In practical terms, that means your site is blazing fast. Speed is a primary factor in keeping your site's readers engaged, browsing, and happy.

Bolt CMS is built on Google's infrastructure with the newest scalable, open-source web technologies. So unlike other CMS options, your site will have the option to gradually grow into a full-featured web application.
